AFODISE: WHAT IS IT?

"You come and see" is the literal meaning of the word “Afodise” in Ibibio (one of the about 450 indigenous languages spoken in Nigeria). African Foundation for Development of Infrastructure and Social Services in Communities (AFODISE) began operating informally as a family charity as far back as 1955. It is now a non-governmental, not-for-profit, non-political, organisation formally registered with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission, registration number 10306. The focus of our activities revolve around usefully harnessing, mobilising, and enhancing the largely untapped energies and resources of the rural people in pursuit of establishing sustainable development of basic infrastructure and social service that will put the people at the centre and aim at improving their quality of life in all perspectives. It is the belief of AFODISE that whatever development made should be sustainable and should meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.


Our Vision

To assist underserved communities in creating SUSTAINED HUMAN AND MATERIAL DEVELOPMENT so as to successfully utilize the relatively hidden potentials of these communities to achieve the desired economic, socio-political and cultural emancipation needed by the entire African society.


Aims and Objectives

• To eliminate poverty and engender truly egalitarian principles in the African society.
• To provide underserved people with employment opportunities and career advancement.
• To optimally utilize the latent energies and reserves in African communities.
• To ignite and sustain economic growth in communities through result oriented mobilization of human resources.
• To initiate, foster and aid in the development and growth of various community development efforts.
• To protect our natural resource base by educating the rural people to be less destructive of their natural resources and reduce environmental degradation.
• To eliminate diabolical cultural instincts and behaviors inimical to modern development.


Our core values and believes

* Strong commitment to our work
* Simple approach in meeting the needs of our clients
* Honesty, Integrity and excellence
* Building relationships with our clients and business partners.
