The African Foundation for the Development of Infrastructure and Social Services in Communities (AFODISE) is committed to assisting underserved communities in creating holistic and sustainable Human & Material Development by igniting and successfully utilising the relatively hidden potentials of the people to achieve their desired socio-political & economic emancipation. It is the belief of AFODISE that whatever development made should be sustainable and should meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. Our vision is to assist underserved communities in creating sustained human and material development so as to successfully utilise the relatively hidden potentials of African communities to achieve the desired economic, socio-political and cultural emancipation needed by the entire African society.
