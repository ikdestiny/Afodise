WHAT WE DO

OUR PROGRAMMES, SCOPE, AND ACTIVITIES
HEALTH CARE
Primary health centers
• Out-Patient Dispensary
• Pharmacy
• Preventive Health Education with emphasis on vaccinations and STDs/HIV-AIDS Screening
• Rehabilitation and Counseling Programs

Maternal/Child health centers
• Immunization Centre
• Vocational Training
• Child care services
• Orphanage
• Old People and Half-Way Homes

Cottage hospitals
• Out/In patient Care
• Development of medical prosthesis and basic ambulatory machinery
• Development of local herbal preparations

Healthcare Cooperation and Network Programs
• Project HELP (Health for Everyone, Less Poverty)
• PrisonFriendship League Prisoners Lean On Me(PLOM)-Reform/Rehab/Reintegrate.

ENERGY
• Hydroelectricity
• Development of Solar/Gas energy, Hydrocarbon Fuel (coal, Kerosene, etc.)

VOLUNTEER PROGRAMME
Volunteer Scheme for Nigerians and other nationals interested in hastening the pace of sustained development by
offering volunteer services to AFODISE in their areas(s) of interest or specialization.

WATER
• Provision of suitable pipe borne water (e.g. Boreholes)
• Irrigation of farming
• Development of distribution networks from natural sources like streams, rivers, etc.

FOOD
• Development and improvement of farming methods and technology including: preparation, storage, preservation, and presentation of food

HOUSING
• Development of construction material from locally available raw materials (modified bricks, thatch, etc.)
• Rural Mapping/Country Planning
• Construction and maintenance of drainage systems

EDUCATION
• Child/Adult Education
• Construction and equipping of science and technical laboratories
• Provision of sports and other extra-curricular facilities
• Emphasizing the need for formal education as a basis for real life behaviors
• Cultivation of a ‘maintenance culture’
